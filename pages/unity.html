What is unity

Unity is a popular tool allowing developers to create games from scratch, using various assets, some of which are available for free on the Unity Asset Store.

Unity is, IMHO, a good starting point for anyone looking to dabble in game development for the first time. Unity is by no means the only option on the market. Engines like Godot or Game Maker may be easier to understand at first, but I find Unity to be right in the middle in terms of ease of use and customizaiblity.

Some incredible games are built with Unity, such as Escape from Tarkov, Ori and the Blind Forest or Hollow Knight.
